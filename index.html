About

I'm your typical munchkin. I do my best to min-max everything I can, and applying this special know-how to Scalr. When not being a complete nerd, I run the Silicon Valley Cloud Computing Group, consisting of over 6,000 members who meet monthly to trade war stories from operating infrastructure.

Prior to founding Scalr, I had a social life and enjoyed skiing, juggling, and getting my butt kicked playing rugby.
